Mumbai Police gives clean chit to Nana Patekar, files 'B summary's report. 'B summary' report is filed when police can not find evidence in support of the complaint and are unable to continue the investigation.

However, complainant Tanushree Dutta is in no mood to back down. 
Slamming "corrupt police force and legal system", Tanushree Dutta now plans to challenge the police report.
