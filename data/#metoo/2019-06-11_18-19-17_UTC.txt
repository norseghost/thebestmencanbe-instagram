From Mindy to Priyanka to Lilly, South Asian women are becoming a force in Hollywood. But there’s also formidable talent making things happen behind the scenes whose stories we’ll be sharing this month. ⁣
⁣
Indian journalist, Meher Tatna, became president of the Hollywood Foreign Press Association (best known for hosting the Golden Globe awards) in the wake of the #MeToo and #TimesUp movement. When actresses wore black to the Golden Globes in a show of silent protest, Meher wore red. ⁣

As you’ll read in our interview, going against the grain is familiar territory for Meher who chose a career in the arts rather than the one her parents wanted for her.
