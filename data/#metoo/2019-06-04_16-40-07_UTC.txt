One hundred years ago #OTD, Congress passed the 19th amendment, which granted women the right to vote. It was ratified the following year. 
To celebrate this milestone anniversary of this historic event, we are offering a year of programming and special exhibits through our “Finding Her Voice” program. We also will have a special exhibit, “The Women of Spiegel Grove,” opening July 12. 
Learn more here: https://www.rbhayes.org/news/2019/04/24/general/finding-her-voice-includes-exhibits-programs-that-highlight-women-s-history/
