How do you see yourself? Do you think you are strong, brave, caring, loving? Or maybe you see yourself as an insecure man - not that worthy of love?
.
What do you think others think about you? Do you think they see you the same way as you see yourself? .
What others think about us should never be what defines our identity, but it can help us with some insight about ourselves. Maybe you picture yourself as a caring man, and your coworkers see you as somehow selfish. Or maybe you don't think you are that great of a man and your family and friends see you as the greatest. .
This is one big quality of great men. Asking for constructive feedback from the ones you make life with takes a lot of courage and maturity, and it can help us SO MUCH on growing. Dare asking!

 Lo que otros piensan de nosotros nunca debe ser lo que define nuestra identidad, pero puede ayudarnos a comprendernos mejor.  Tal vez te imaginas a ti mismo como un hombre cariñoso, y tus compañeros de trabajo te ven como alguien egoísta.  O tal vez no creas que eres un gran hombre y tu familia y amigos te ven como el mejor. .
 Esta es una cualidad muy importante de un gran hombre. Pedir retroalimentación constructiva de aquellos con los que haces vida requiere de mucho valor y madurez, y nos puede ayudar TANTO a crecer. ¡Atrévete a preguntar! . . .
