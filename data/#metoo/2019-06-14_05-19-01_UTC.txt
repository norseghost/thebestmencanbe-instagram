Meet Raised Pinay Rose. @rsgnrs

Rose Generoso is a second-gen Pilipinx-American femme, musician, and weaver. She has spent most of her life on Lenape (Canarsee and Rockaway) lands while bearing roots hailing from Pangasinan and Masbate. Rose is also part of the Walang Hiya NYC (@walanghiyanyc) collective.
