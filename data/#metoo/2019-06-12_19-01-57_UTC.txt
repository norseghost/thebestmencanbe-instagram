Some aggrieved women in Ondo, a South-Western state in Nigeria, took to the streets last month to protest the rising cases of attacks, rituals and kidnapping by suspected cattle herdsmen. In a gender analysis of the impact of Boko Haram Insurgency, a @unwomen representative reveals that an overwhelming majority of women have been displaced, raped and rendered homeless with their children who make up an estimated 1.8 million internally displaced persons (IDP's). Click the link in our bio to read how the human catastrophe endagers women.
