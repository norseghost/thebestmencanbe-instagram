On 11th June 2019, Justice for Lai Dai Han hosted an event in Westminster, on Ending Sexual Violence in Conflict; this event was the second in a series on the widespread proliferation of sexual violence in conflict.

Nobel Peace Prize Laureate Nadia Murad headlined the event and was joined by Baroness Emma Nicholson along with victims of sexual violence.
