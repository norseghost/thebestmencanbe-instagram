The @tblightning honored Melissa Mihok, our Founder and CEO/Executive Director, as the 303rd Lightning Community Hero in 2017. This past weekend, Melissa joined the other 365 Community Heroes in Tampa Bay for a reunion celebration. Over the past 8 years, $18.3 million dollars has been raised for over 497 non-profits, including Heels to Heal. We are honored to be part of this amazing community!
