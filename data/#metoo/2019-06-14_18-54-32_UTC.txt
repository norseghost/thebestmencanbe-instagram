ABFF About Women panel (Co-sponsored by Accenture and Hilton)

ABOUT WOMENis a panel that will bring together a multigenerational group of women including filmmakers, actors, and executives to discuss topics, issues, and opportunities specific to their experiences, including gendered language in the workplace, wage disparity, the #METOO movement, and other inequities that adversely affect women of color in Hollywood. In addition, this panel will also offer insight and define clear pathways to success for aspiring female artists and executives within the entertainment industry. 
Panelists: Tracy Oliver (Girls Trip), La La Anthony (Power), Iyanla Vanzant (Iyanla:Fix My Life) and Connie Orlando (EVP, Head of Programming of BET Networks) 
Moderator: Jemele Hill (The Atlantic)
