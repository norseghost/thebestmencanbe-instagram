Today I commemorate 7 years of the march that was organized in memory of my sister and other women who were murdered by their beloved partners... When you transform the pain of loosing someone into a fearless power combine with fierce creativity, you might be surprised by the strength you’ve been holding up. 
Es cliché pero... Transformar lo malo en algo bueno... es difícil pero no imposible... cuando hay amor, perseverancia, esperanza y sobretodo disciplina se logran cosas que uno nunca se imagina.

A nosotros por ejemplo, nos sorprendió el sistema judicial colombiano... dándole al asesino de mi hermana 45 años de cárcel.
