‪One year‬
‪The marker of time passing‬ ‪

Such a small thing‬
‪And yet,‬
‪It is tied to those memories‬
‪The ones that wreak havoc‬
‪And suffocate ‬ ‪

A miles stone should have no right‬
‪To make me feel this vividly‬ ‪
.
