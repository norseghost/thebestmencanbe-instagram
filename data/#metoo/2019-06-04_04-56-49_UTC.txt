Transitions can be ...uncomfortable. ⠀
Transitioning from single adulthood to parenthood. Takes adjustments. Transitioning from married to single. Again, takes adjustments. What about transitions of our environs? Seeing habitat destroyed for the dollar. Parking lots replacing riparian zones.  Old growth trees removed for the ease of humans and that new bigger house. ⠀ ⠀

This requires us to make adjustments. and those humans witnessing can deal with some mighty feelings, if they are woke and paying attention. Hearing the screams of the hawks as their beloved nesting grounds, vantage points in our neighborhood are systematically removed. Watching the loose dirt left behind by poor construction practices turn into mud in the rain, and run down the gutter into the river and our drinking water.⠀ ⠀

How do we adjust? How do we find balance? How do we make peace? Do we? ⠀

Carrying a torch in the dark can be a challenge. Today, I am working on anger. Frustration. check my blog out to learn more about how I am finding a level of light and connectedness despite it all. www.lynellgarfield.com Our hearts and those of our children need to be buoyed up, despite everything happening on the news and down the block. Bring it all back. It is time. ⠀

On this new moon today, let us dance.  Let us know, this is time for meditating on our dreams and finding our desires, then setting our intentions. I do not choose anger for this new moon...in my book, I find cooling Anger is supported by geranium, thyme, ylang yang, white fir, cardamom, forgive oils. I will carry some of these today... and will be gentle on myself and my feelings. I will feel vulnerable and use my voice, I will dance and dream. I will be Sending love. And always, sending light. <3 ⠀
