Conductor & Producer, Amy Andersson and a team of Hollywood female film composers have joined forces to create a one of a kind, non profit musical experience, which features thrilling live-to-film visuals and a full symphony orchestra. 
The event will honor the strength and heroism of global activists. Women Warriors: The Voices of Change will premiere at Lincoln Center, NYC September 20, 2019.
