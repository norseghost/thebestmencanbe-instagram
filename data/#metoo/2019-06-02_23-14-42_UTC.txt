Join us and @therealarianasanchez as she’ll host a Bold Talk—Bold Talks are monthly get-togethers for trauma survivors (domestic violence, sexual abuse, etc.) and their families, friends, and significant others.  We are here to provide support to you (the survivor) AND those that support you.  Bold Talks is all about bringing you empowering resources to help you heal, bring clarity, relief, peace, and love as you navigate the healing journey. Join us and @therealarianasanchez and @boldascension as we create a space for healing.
