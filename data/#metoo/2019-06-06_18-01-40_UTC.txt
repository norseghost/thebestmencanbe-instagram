HELMUT NEWTON EXHIBITION 3/3. Charlottenburg, Berlin, Germany.

What would #metoo say about it?

The exhibition in Museum für Fotografie am Bahnhof Zoo opens today, 6th of June 2019. Three parts: 1. The pictures from the famous 20-years-old SUMO-book from Taschen Verlag. 2. Three Boys from Pasadena: Three former assistants of Newton, who did their own career. And 3. the Highlight: First-time-exhibition of the private collection of Helmut and June Newton: August Sander, Albert Watson, Irving Penn, Man Ray, Mapplethorpe and many others, a who-is-who of the 20.-century-photography.
