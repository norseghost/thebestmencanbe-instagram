Me(a)t Donald Trump
« He would step in front of each girl and look you over from head to toe like we were just meat, we were just sexual objects, that we were not people » Samantha Holvey (CNN October 2016)
That’s how it started, my work on #Trump sexual misconduct allegations using portraits of women who have accused him. I created this hanging picture, in silicone and polyurethane foam, so that it can be viewed from both sides. One side shows the solidarity of women who accuse him in one single drawing, while the other side shows the repulsive and dirty face of Trump.
This piece is to show solidarity for women who have shared similar accounts over far too many years, so that we can be done with the pork industry.

Holvey 
C’est comme ça que mon travail a commencé sur les allégations pour agressions sexuelles concernant Trump par l’emploi des portraits des femmes qui l'ont accusé. Ce tableau à suspendre en silicone et mousse de polyuréthane est conçu pour être vu des deux côtés. Un côté montre la solidarité des femmes qui l’accusent à travers un dessin d’un seul tenant, tandis que l’autre côté montre le visage repoussant de Trump.
