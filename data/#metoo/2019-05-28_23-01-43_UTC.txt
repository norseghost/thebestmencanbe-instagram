29th May - Photographing or recording sex acts without consent is IPSV.
Photographing or recording sex acts without consent is IPSV. Sharing these intimate images without consent in order to shame is sometimes called ‘Revenge Porn’. The act of sharing or threatening to share images, visual recordings or photo shopped images is now a criminal offence In Queensland.
