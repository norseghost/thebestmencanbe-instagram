“Nobody can go back and start a new beginning, but anyone can start today and make a new ending.” - Maria Robinson ✨
Everyone goes through hardships in life that may make them lose faith, want to give up, or believe that things simply won’t get better. 
Although it may be difficult to believe at first, in due time, you won’t be in a bad situation forever. With that, your hardships in life can be taken as lessons, that you can bring with you into a brighter future! Remember, that even though you can’t change the past, you can grab ahold of and create an amazing future for yourself. ✨
