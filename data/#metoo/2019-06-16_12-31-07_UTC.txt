Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez and Ayanna Pressley worked with a handful of other Democratic women lawmakers to introduce the "Affordability is Access" act that would require insurance company to cover oral contraception and have it available over the counter without the need for a doctor's prescription. They want people to be able to access birth control easily and affordabily.
