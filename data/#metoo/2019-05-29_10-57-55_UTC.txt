GENDERED TOYS 🤖🧚🏻‍♀️ I needed a minute to scrape my brain off the floor when I read this headline this morning 🤯 The DM present a piece of research showing that men (capital letters) ARE better than women at map-reading. The article blatantly points out that this is because boys typically play with Lego but fails to mention the societal gender norm at the root of this problem. Printed advertising, marketing and TV adverts are guilty of gendering and stereotyping toys to the extent that in 2019 there are STILL separate ‘for boys’ and ‘for girls’ departments.
