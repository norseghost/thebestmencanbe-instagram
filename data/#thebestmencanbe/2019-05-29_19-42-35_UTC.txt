Validating a new brand campaign can take time, but it is crucial when taking a stand. #Gillette started off the year with their #TheBestMenCanBe campaign and was met with some criticism and calls of virtue signaling. This weekend they followed up with the next video of the campaign, #FirstShave, where a father helps his transgender son shave. The video has garnered great respect for the brand - by committing to their stance they are changing how we think of Gillette and its products.
